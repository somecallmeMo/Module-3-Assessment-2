About this item

A crunch roll is a type of sushi roll that's not traditionally Japanese, but was invented in Los Angeles and adapted to Western tastes. It's characterized by its crunchy tempura flakes on the outside and eel sauce on top. This is one of the best types of sushi for newbies as it is deep fried, not raw.
